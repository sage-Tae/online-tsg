## Motivation

v2.1.8 met the EJOR 30-page cap but still contained desk-check
violations that would have auto-returned the submission before
any reviewer touched it:

- **Abstract contained mathematical formulae** (`$r^{\ast\ast}$`,
  `$\min_i \delta_i / \cstar(N)$`, `$r^{\ast\ast\ast}$`,
  `$O(n^{-1/2})$`, `$\bar r^{\ast}=4.351$`, etc.) — EJOR
  explicitly forbids formulae in abstracts.
- **Abstract referenced theorem numbers** via `\ref` (Theorem 11,
  Proposition 12, Corollary 13, Theorem 14, Remark 14, Observation
  15) — EJOR abstracts must be self-contained and readable without
  the rest of the paper.
- **Abstract used undefined abbreviations on first use**: TSG, LP,
  TSP, BHH — EJOR requires that abbreviations be defined in full
  the first time they appear.
- **Keywords were 7, not 5** — EJOR caps keywords at 5, one of
  which must come from the EJOR official keyword list.
- **Figure 2 caption included "see Appendix C"** — leftover from
  before the v2.1.8 EJOR-compliance split; the figure has no
  Appendix to point at anymore.
- **REPRODUCIBILITY.md Data-Files table listed four execution logs
  that are not in the ZIP**, and an orphan "Design Documents"
  section listed three internal working files also not in the ZIP.

All four are mechanical-check items at the EJOR Editorial Manager
stage, so fixing them before the next iteration is a precondition
